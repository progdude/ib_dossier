Instructions

This is a motion active game. In order to play this game, make sure you have a computer with a webcam and a small magenta/pink slice of paper. That piece of paper will be your tracking device. The objective of this game is to kill the monsters, by dragging the cursor above it. You have 20 seconds to kill as many monsters as you can. Good Luck!
